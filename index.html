Object-Aware Gaussian Splatting for Robotic Manipulation

Real-time Reconstruction of Dynamic Scenes

Our method reconstructs the dynamic scene in real time at 30 Hz with fast moving objects and robot actions, with only three RGB-D cameras.

Persistent Semantic Features

We extract relevant semantic features at the first time step, and we can obtain peristent semantic features during dynamic movements in the scene due to explicit tracking of the underlying Gaussians. As an example, we extract the segmentation map at the first time step and show the peristent segmentation map.

Language-conditioned Dynamic Grasping

Our dynamic and semantic scene representation enables language-conditioned grasping, even if the objects are moving in the scene!

Representation for Behavior Cloning Policies

Semantic information, 3D representation, and high frequency perception seem to be an impossible triangle... But with our representation, all three features are achieved at the same time! We can use our representation to train behavior cloning policies.
